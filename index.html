Privacy Policy for HushTone

Effective date: 17 May 2026

HushTone is provided by Hyper Focused Apps.

HushTone is a sound masking app that provides background sounds, tone presets, frequency calibration, and a custom notch sound for relaxation, focus, sleep, and personal sound masking.

Important Notice

HushTone is provided for general wellness, relaxation, focus, sleep, and personal sound masking purposes only.

HushTone is not a medical device and is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, prevent, monitor, or manage tinnitus, hearing loss, ear conditions, sleep disorders, anxiety, stress, or any other medical or health condition.

HushTone is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, hearing assessment, therapy, or care from a qualified healthcare professional. You should not rely on HushTone to make medical decisions.

Information We Collect

HushTone does not require users to create an account and does not ask for personal contact information such as name, email address, or phone number.

HushTone does not intentionally collect medical records, hearing test results, diagnoses, treatment information, or other clinical health information from users.

Custom Notch Frequency

HushTone allows you to select a frequency for the custom notch sound. This selected frequency is stored locally on your device and is used only to create your custom notch sound.

The selected frequency is not linked to an account and is not sent to Hyper Focused Apps, Firebase Analytics, Google AdMob, or any external server by HushTone.

You can change the selected frequency in the app or remove it by resetting app data or uninstalling the app.

HushTone does not guarantee that any sound, tone, frequency, notch setting, timer, or feature will reduce tinnitus, improve hearing, improve sleep, relieve symptoms, prevent symptoms, improve wellbeing, or provide any health benefit.

Hearing Safety

Listening to audio at high volume for extended periods may damage your hearing. Use HushTone at a comfortable, low volume and avoid prolonged exposure to loud sounds.

Stop using the app and seek medical advice if you experience discomfort, pain, worsening symptoms, dizziness, ringing, pressure, or any concern about your hearing.
