Privacy Policy for HushTone

Effective date: 17 May 2026

HushTone is provided by Hyper Focused Apps.

HushTone is a sound masking app that provides background sounds, tone presets, frequency calibration, and a custom notch sound for relaxation, focus, sleep, and tinnitus sound masking. HushTone is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

Information We Collect

HushTone does not require users to create an account and does not ask for personal contact information such as name, email address, or phone number.

Custom Notch Frequency

HushTone allows you to select a frequency for the custom notch sound. This selected frequency is stored locally on your device and is used only to create your custom notch sound.

The selected frequency is not linked to an account and is not sent to Hyper Focused Apps, Firebase Analytics, Google AdMob, or any external server by HushTone.

Automatically Collected Information

HushTone uses third-party services that may automatically collect certain information to support advertising, analytics, payments, crash reporting, app reliability, security, and app improvement.

This information may include device information, operating system version, app opens, app interactions, settings interactions, purchase or subscription events, payment flow events, approximate location, advertising identifiers, app instance identifiers, diagnostics, crash logs, event timestamps, and other technical information.

Third-Party Services

HushTone may use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob to show advertisements
  • Firebase Analytics to understand app usage, feature interactions, purchases, subscriptions, payment flow events, app performance, and product behaviour
  • Firebase Crashlytics or similar crash reporting tools, if enabled, to identify crashes and technical issues

These services process data according to their own privacy policies and terms.

Advertising

HushTone may show ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and process data such as device identifiers, advertising identifiers, approximate location, ad interactions, app activity, diagnostics, and other technical information to provide, measure, and improve advertising.

Depending on your device settings and applicable law, ads may be personalised or non-personalised. You may be able to control ad personalisation through your device settings or Google ad settings.

Analytics

HushTone may use Firebase Analytics or similar analytics tools to understand general app usage and improve the app.

Analytics events may include information about app opens, screen views, feature usage, settings interactions, purchases, subscriptions, payment flow events, errors, performance, and other interactions with the app.

This information may include device information, operating system version, approximate location, app instance identifiers, advertising identifiers, diagnostics, event timestamps, product or subscription identifiers, purchase status, currency, price, and related transaction metadata where applicable.

We use this information to understand app performance, improve features, fix issues, measure monetisation, prevent misuse, and make product decisions.

We do not use Firebase Analytics to collect your name, email address, phone number, or selected notch frequency.

Crash Reporting

If crash reporting is enabled, HushTone may collect crash logs, diagnostics, device information, operating system version, and technical information related to app crashes. This information helps us fix bugs and improve app stability.

Precise Location

HushTone does not collect precise real-time location information from your device.

Health and Medical Disclaimer

HushTone does not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent tinnitus or any other health condition. It only provides background sounds that some users may find helpful for masking tinnitus. Please consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical advice.

Children’s Privacy

HushTone is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Data Retention

HushTone does not maintain user accounts or personal profiles.

Your selected notch frequency is stored locally on your device and can be changed in the app or removed by resetting app data or uninstalling the app.

Data collected by third-party services such as Google AdMob, Firebase Analytics, and crash reporting tools is handled according to those services’ own retention and privacy practices.

Your Choices

You can stop all data collection by uninstalling the app.

You may also be able to limit ad tracking, reset your advertising ID, or control ad personalisation through your device settings.

Security

We use reasonable measures to avoid unnecessary data collection and protect information processed through the app. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.
